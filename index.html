Why Ghost?

If you have Ghost, you have everything

Or a bit of everything, at least. Some have described their music as the love child of ABBA and Iron Maiden, others have named it "Scooby-Doom". In truth, their style is hard to pin down but maybe that's what makes them so appealing. They blend catchy pop melodies with heavy metal, creating a unique sound with strong hooks, evolving theatricality, elaborate stage shows, and clever, lore-driven lyrics that appeal to both metalheads and mainstream rock fans.

Tobias Forge, the mastermind, crafts compelling narratives and consistently reinvents the band's look and sound with each album cycle, drawing from diverse influences like Blue Öyster Cult, Alice Cooper, and classic rock. Their live shows are nothing short of spectacular, with creative storytelling, high production values and serious musicianship.

Formed in 2006, the band have released six studio albums, which have earned them international recognition. The band's sixth studio album, Skeletá, was released in 2025; it subsequently became their first number one on the US Billboard 200 chart.

About Tobias Forge

A Swedish musician, Forge is the frontman, primary songwriter, and sole continuous member of the masked rock band Ghost, performing live as their vocalist under the stage names Papa Emeritus, Cardinal Copia, and Papa Perpetua.
